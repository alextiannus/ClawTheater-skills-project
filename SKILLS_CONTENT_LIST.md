A curated collection of writing and content creation skills for producing high-quality prose, copy, documentation, and creative work.

## 1. Stop Slop
**Description:** Remove AI writing patterns from prose.

**Use When:** Drafting, editing, or reviewing text to eliminate predictable AI tells.

**Introduction:** Eliminate predictable AI writing patterns from prose. Cut filler phrases, break formulaic structures, use active voice, and be specific. Core principles include varying rhythm, trusting readers, and putting them in the room with authentic, human-sounding writing.

**Key Focus:** Directness, rhythm, authenticity, trust, and density of language.

## 4. Copy Editing
**Description:** Systematically improve existing marketing copy through focused editing passes.

**Use When:** User wants to edit, review, or improve existing marketing copy; mentions 'edit this copy,' 'review my copy,' 'copy feedback,' 'proofread,' 'polish this,' or 'make this better.'

**Introduction:** You are an expert copy editor specializing in marketing and conversion copy. Systematically improve existing copy through focused editing passes while preserving the core message. Good copy editing isn't about rewriting — it's about enhancing. Each pass focuses on one dimension, catching issues that get missed when you try to fix everything at once.

**Key Focus:** Seven-sweep framework (clarity, voice & tone, so what, prove it, specificity, urgency, and flow).
